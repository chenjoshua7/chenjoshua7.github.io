About Me

Welcome to my webpage! I'm the furthest thing from a web developer but I wanted to have this space to organize some of the things I have been doing and for you all to get to know me a bit better! Please explore and click around. Don't forget to hit me up on LinkedIn and to check out my Github.

A little about me: I studied Mathematics and minored in Economics at Boston College. I graduated during the COVID pandemic. I taught high shcool mathematics for three years in Brooklyn, New York.

While I loved my time teaching, I realized I still had a lot I wanted to learn. Hence, I am currently getting my Masters in Data Science Methodology from the Barcelona School of Economics. I chose BSE because its curriculum provided both a rigorous fundamental understnading of statistics and mathematics as well as ample opportunities to apply theory in real world contexts. While wrapping up my last term, I am writing my Masters thesis on a performance comparison between Convolusional Neural Networks and Vision Transformers. Additionally, I am currently interning for the Development Impact Evaluation (DIME) of the World Bank.

My areas of interest currently include: Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, Reocommendation Systems, Statistical Inference, and Data Visualization.
