Curse of Dimensionality

The Curse of Dimensionality complicates machine learning, making problems exponentially harder as dimensions increase. Understanding the true posterior distribution in hyperdimensional space is essential for accurate predictions, but as dimensions grow, the complexity of accurately modeling and predicting outcomes significantly increases.
Skills: Data Visualization, Statistics, Python, NumPy, kNN, t-SNE, PCA
