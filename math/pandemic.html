Pandemic Modeling

I have done pandemic modeling in two different circumstances with 2 different approaches. The first time was in Senior Year of my undergraduate program in my Mathematical Modeling course with SIR pandemic modeling. Coincidentally, this was also during COVID.

The second circumstance was in my Networks class in my Masters program from a Networks perspective.

Undergraduate - Markov Chains

In my undergraduate course, we model pandemics with Markov chains. Find an example of one of my old homeworks here.

Typically in SIR pandemic modeling, we want to model the number of people Susceptible, Infected, and Recovered in a given state (hence the name). Below is an example from the problem set listed above. B represents the birthrate.
